The process behind the podcast

Welcome to my podcast called Loremasters. This is a podcast that I did as an assignment for my Digital Media 2260 class at Utah Valley University. The goal of this podcast was to create a professional sounding and looking podcast from the ground up. Utilizing the information we learned about podcasting in this course. We each chose a topic we are interested in and then created two episodes of a podcast pertaining to that topic.
