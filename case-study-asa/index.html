Augmented Reality Restaurant
Menu For Asa Ramen

Mike Dahle

The Problem

Restaurant menus are pretty boring. They haven't changed much over the years other than when some restaurants needed to use QR code menus during the height of the pandemic. Usually when you go out to eat at a restaurant you will get handed a menu that is largely text based and maybe has a few pictures to give you an idea of what the food looks like. But other than that they are pretty boring most of the time. That got me thinking, what if you could see more than just static pictures on your menu? What if you could use your phone to bring your menu to life!?

WHAT CAN WE DO?

Example Menus for Asa Ramen

The Process

For this project I needed to use a website called Zapworks. It is a pretty cool concept that would be perfect for this project. Zapworks allows you to create Zap codes which you can then attach to digital or print based products and then your customers can use Zapworks Zappar App to scan a custom QR code and interact with an augmented reality experience that you create on their website. Now originally I thought it would be really amazing if I could figure out a way to either model or scan each of the dishes that has a code next to it. That way you could scan a code for tonkotsu ramen and see the dish appear on your menu with augmented reality. Unfortunately I dont have the technical skills for that yet. So, I had to settle on making slideshows and videos which you can view and interact with on your phone.

Conclusion

I believe the original concept I had envisioned has a lot of potential. Imagine how cool it would be to see a 3D render of the food you want to order before you order it. The AR experience I was able to create at my current experience level fell short of my own expectations. But that mostly comes down to my inexperience with the software itself. But with all of that said, I want to leave you with one of my favorite AR experiences that I have seen. The video included here is a AR billboard being used to advertise the new Resident Evil show.
